When it comes to unique iced coffee flavors, Victor Allen’s Iced Coffee Snickers is an absolute standout. It combines the smoothness of iced coffee with the nostalgic sweetness of a Snickers candy bar.

INGREDIENTS


INSTRUCTIONS


  1. Chop the Snickers bar into small pieces.
  2. Blend Snickers with iced coffee until smooth.
  3. Add chocolate and caramel syrup to the blend.
  4. Pour over crushed ice in a glass.
  5. Add milk, stir well.
  6. Top with whipped cream if desired.
  7. That's it, Enjoy!
